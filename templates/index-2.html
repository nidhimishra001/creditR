Our Services

Home Loan

CreditR offers Home Loan of up to ₹3.5 crore at an interest rate starting at 7.5%* tenure up to 7 years.

Car Loan

CreditR offers Car Loans up to 100% of on-road price of the car, with attractive interest rates and tenure up to 7 years.

Personal Loan

Collateral-free Personal Loans with EMIs starting from Rs.1400 with tenure of 9 years.

Gold Loan

CreditR Gold Loan can be availed by pledge of gold ornaments including gold coins sold by Banks with minimum paper work and low interest rate.

Education Loan
